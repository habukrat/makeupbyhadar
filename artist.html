Meet Hadar

Makeup By Hadarling was founded in 2018 by Hadar Abukrat. Always having a passion for all things beauty and makeup, Hadar decided to become a certified makeup artist. Since then, she has been working as a freelance artist, mainly serving the Los Angeles area. She is highly skilled in makeup for all occasion such as wedding, special events, and photoshoots. She listens to her clients to help them create a look that makes them feel comfortable and beautiful!
